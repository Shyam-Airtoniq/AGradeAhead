Our Approach

From Fundamentals to Real-World Success.

Education is the key to a successful life. The benefits of education are limitless for a child’s future successes. At A Grade Ahead, we have a proven approach to help your child reach his or her goals. Our curriculum increases in difficulty each month as it guides students to fully grasp critical concepts and skills. Your child will receive the individual attention that tutoring students receive but also have the added benefit of classroom interaction. By attending weekly classes and completing our worksheets, your child will have an academic advantage in school and be positioned to excel in life.

Practice Makes Progress

A blended environment for real, weekly progress.

Unlike the competition, your student will learn in a blended environment. Unlike traditional tutoring, our teachers will teach a lesson each week. Your child will interact with the teacher and classmates to understand new concepts related to math and English. In class, your child will receive monthly curriculum that includes:

  • A written lesson each week explaining a new concept

  • Student practice questions

  • Three additional days of practice material per week, either online or on paper

  • Hands-on activities that relate key concepts to real-life situations

  • Auto-graded online homework or comprehensive answer keys for traditional homework

With teacher and peer interaction, your child will learn a new concept each week and practice it with teacher assistance. For the rest of the week, your child will have homework to solidify the concepts that were taught in class.
