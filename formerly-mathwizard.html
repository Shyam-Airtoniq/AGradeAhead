The Rebrand

Same trusted program. Much bigger purpose.

MathWizard is now A Grade Ahead. We renamed because our after-school programme now features so much more than just math tutoring. Today, our curriculum includes English, science, hands-on learning camps, and standardized testing prep.

And if you don’t live near one of our 30+ enrichment academies, our convenient Enrichment at Home programme delivers the same quality experience straight to your kitchen table.

If you were looking for MathWizard, A Grade Ahead is where you want to be now — for math, and so much more.

Our Story

How MathWizard became A Grade Ahead.

When Meenal and Parag Singhal founded MathWizard in 2001, they would never have imagined they’d outgrow the name. But that’s exactly what happened. Originally, the couple offered only math curriculum, delivered to students at home through their Tutoring by Mail program. Over the next fifteen years, the company expanded and the name MathWizard didn’t articulate everything they offered. They added English, then science, then enrichment camps like MathVenture, Eco Explorers, and Robotics.

Some students — and even staff — felt a disconnection to the name MathWizard, especially those teaching English or science. So the company looked at every option to see if a different name would suit them better. Parag came up with A Grade Ahead, and it felt right. We want to challenge our students not only to achieve A’s in school, but to be at the head of their class — literally a grade ahead of their peers.

Despite how much we love the new name, moving away from MathWizard wasn’t easy — it was catchy, and we all loved it. You’ll still occasionally hear a parent or student refer to us as MathWizard, and that’s ok. We consider it a compliment.

Whether you still call us MathWizard or have embraced the name A Grade Ahead, the company is still owned and managed by the Singhals, still offers curriculum that’s second to none, and still has the same goals for our families. We remain the same family-oriented company we have always been.

Meenal & Parag Singhal — Co-founders, since 2001
