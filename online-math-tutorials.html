Quick Tips

A few things to know before you start.

Watch the videos below with your student. Here are the key tips covered:

  • Use the right arrow to start an activity or move to the next question.

  • Hints are available on most questions. They’re optional — there’s no penalty for opening them.

  • Some questions show a pencil image. Click it to open a workspace where your student can show their work.

  • After you press Submit, you’ll see immediately if it’s correct. Click Show Explanation or Show Correct Answer for the rationale.

  • The last page provides a summary of your scores, including percent correct and time taken.

  • At the end of the week your student receives a quiz or test. There’s a time limit, and unanswered questions are marked incorrect.
